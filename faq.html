What are the PD attendance and participation policies?

These policies apply to all members of the DAS staff (including apprenticeship interpreters and students) wishing to participate in our program. They are in place to ensure the quality of our Professional Development Program.

  • DAS PDP Attendance Policy
  • Attendance at any registered PD event is mandatory unless previously excused with valid reason
    • This includes ISTs, workshops, and formally established small groups
  • Attendance is reported to the managers weekly for ISTs and directly following workshops
  • Anyone wishing to receive RID CEUs will only be excused from one IST or small group session (with prior notification) and still be awarded CEUs
    • Individuals missing 1 IST session will sign an Activity Report form with an appropriately reduced number of CEUs
  • Anyone missing more than one IST or small group session whether excused or not will not be eligible to receive CEUs
  • Small groups can arrange to make up time lost due to one or more membersâ€™ inability to attend with valid reason
    • This make up session must be agreed upon by all group members and be reflected on the attendance sheet
  • Workshop attendance will continue to be monitored and CEU credit will only be awarded to those present for the entire duration of the event
  • Anyone wishing to withdraw from a PDP event prior to or during the event can do so with manager approval
    • Intent to withdraw should be communicated via email to the event facilitator, your manager, coordinator and the PD coordinator

DAS PDP Participation Guidelines:

Professional Development activities are designed with you in mind. Our program offers DAS staff the opportunity to take time out from providing their service to actually working on developing the skills necessary to provide the high level of service we are committed to providing to the institute and to one another.

  • Those wishing to engage in professional development activities offered through the PDP agree to:
  • Prior to the PD (workshop, IST, small group) event:
    1. Discuss with their manager the PDP offerings and how they might serve to fulfill expectations for professional growth
    2. Attend to registration timelines and register in a timely manner
    3. Communicate with their coordinators to ensure their availability for the duration of the event
    4. Be sure you are clear on the learning objectives outlined for any event you register for
    5. Complete any preparatory work assigned by the facilitators
  • During the PD (workshop, IST, small group) event:
    1. Arrive on time for the event
    2. Sign in
    3. Be present and ready to fully participate in all activities and discussions
    4. Have completed any "homework" assigned by the facilitator(s)
    5. Silence cell phones and put away all digital devices not in use for the actual PD event
    6. No sleeping
    7. Complete pretest, post-test and evaluation for every event
      • CEUs and participation certificates will not be given to anyone not completing these documents

Special Note: If you are unable to attend a PD event (workshop, IST, small group) for any reason, you must contact the event facilitator(s), your manager, and the PDP coordinator prior to the event. Attendance is reported to managers following every event. Please see the DAS PDP Attendance Policy for more information.
